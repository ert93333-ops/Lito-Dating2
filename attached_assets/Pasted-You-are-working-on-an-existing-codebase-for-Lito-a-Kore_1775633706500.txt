You are working on an existing codebase for Lito, a Korean-Japanese cross-border dating app.

IMPORTANT:
- Do NOT rewrite the whole app
- Do NOT replace existing chat, profile, or onboarding systems unless necessary

Your task:
Design an MVP-ready “AI Mutual Interest Signal” system for Lito.

This feature must NOT be framed as:
- “love score”
- “exact attraction score”
- “the other person definitely likes you”

Instead, it must be framed as:
- Partner Receptivity Score (PRS)
- Confidence Score (CS)
- stage-aware conversational insight
- explanation-based coaching

The system should estimate:
“How likely the other person is to continue engaging positively and move toward the next interaction stage, based on observed conversation signals.”

==================================================
PRODUCT PRINCIPLES
==================================================

1. This is NOT a mind-reading feature
2. This is a probabilistic signal system, not a certainty engine
3. The UI must avoid overclaiming
4. The model should provide reasons, not only a score
5. Use LLMs as semantic feature extractors when useful, not as the final decision-maker
6. The system must work for Korean-Japanese cross-border communication
7. Cultural differences must be handled through calibration, not stereotypes
8. Reply speed should be evaluated relative to the partner’s own baseline when possible

==================================================
CORE OUTPUTS TO DESIGN
==================================================

Design these outputs:

1. Partner Receptivity Score (PRS)
- range: 0 to 100
- meaning: estimated current receptivity / positive engagement likelihood

2. Confidence Score (CS)
- range: 0 to 100
- meaning: how reliable the estimate is given message volume, consistency, and signal quality

3. Conversation Stage
- opening
- discovery
- escalation

4. Reason Codes / Explainability Layer
Examples:
- “They frequently ask follow-up questions”
- “They respond faster than their usual pace”
- “They continue your topics naturally”
- “There is not enough data yet”
- “Signals are mixed”

==================================================
STAGE DEFINITIONS
==================================================

Propose implementation logic for stage detection such as:

Opening:
- fewer than 30 total turns OR within the first 72 hours

Discovery:
- 30 to 120 turns AND no clear progression event yet

Escalation:
- call suggestion, date suggestion, concrete availability sharing, or strong next-step planning signals

You may refine the thresholds based on the current data model.

Design the system around these signal groups:

1. Responsiveness

2. Reciprocity

3. Linguistic Matching

5. Warmth / Other-Focus / Authenticity

- availability sharing
- call or date acceptance / openness

7. Penalty Signals
- early oversharing

- non-contingent topic switching
- scam-risk-related behavior
